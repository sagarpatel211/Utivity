Timer/Dashboard/History Help

How do I use the Timer?

First, you must direct yourself to the tab with the clock-looking icon. This tab is where your data is collected from. In order to use it, you must press start and the stopwatch will start counting upwards. Once you finish your productivity session, you must press the submit button, which will complete your timer and store it to be used in the dashboard and historical data page. IMPORTANT: Once you begin your timer, you cannot go to another tab in the app or else the stopwatch will reset. You can run the timer in the background, but switching through tabs after starting a timer will result in the closing and deletion of the session.

How can I access the Dashboard?

To check the Dashboard, you must click the home icon in the Navigation bar on the left or click the logo in the top left. This will redirect you to the home page where you can access all the important statistics about your work throughout periods of time and your workflow ever since you started using the app.

How am I supposed to check my historical data?

You can check your historical data by going to the tab with the database icon. This tab is the history tab and it's where you will be able to access previous data. On this page you cannot make any alterations or make any edits/deletes.

To-Do List Help

How do I use the ToDo List?

- To start, go to the todo list page by clicking the icon that has a square with a check mark inside it. From there you can add items to your list.

- When you are on the to-do page and want to add an item, type it into the input box and press the submit button. This will add that item to the bottom of your list.

- After you have created a list of items but want to edit or remove items, you must simply click on top of the input box with the task, edit the task, and press the edit button to save changes.

- Once you click off your todo list and go to another tab such as the timer or dashboard, your list will remain the same because it is linked to your account in the database.

Settings Help

What can I do on the Settings page?

- The settings page is dedicated to keeping your account up to date and to assist with logging out in special circumstances.

- The first feature of the settings page is the ability to change your account's profile picture. To change your profile picture, you must upload an image file (PNG/JPG) by clicking on the "choose file" button. Once you have your file uploaded, you can click the 'Update profile picture' button which will allow your profile picture to be updated.

- The second feature on the settings page is the addition of another logout button. This is an alternate button from the one in the bottom left corner in case users don't know what the button means. This button is useful because it allows for the user to visually see how they can logout without needing to interpret the icons.
